All New Hires are required to take and successfully pass a series of pre-employment certifipathions before they may begin work at Material In Motion.

Before we begin...


1. Select your Learning Path on the right of the screen

2. Review your Certifipathions Guidelines. These intructions will appear in this section for each Learning Path

3. There is a time limit set for each learning path. You must complete the training within the set time frame

4. Click “Check Your Scores” at any time to view your results for each test


Please see a member of staff if you have problems with the certifipathions. Let’s get started!
